What is the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025?

The TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025 is conducted by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission for the direct recruitment of candidates to various non-interview posts such as the Assistant Engineer, Junior Manager, Senior Accountant, Librarian, Junior Planner, Project Officer, Computer Programmer, Systems Manager, etc. included in the various Combined Technical Services in the Tamil Nadu Government. This year, the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025 Official Notification was released on 21 May 2025 as per the TNPSC Annual Planner 2025.

When is the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Written Exam 2025 scheduled to be held?

The TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Written Exam 2025 is scheduled to be held from 4 August 2025 to 10 August 2025 according to the TNPSC Annual Planner 2025 and the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025 Official Notification PDF.

What are the posts included in the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025?

The following posts included in the Combined Technical Services of various departments and organisations in the Tamil Nadu Government are listed in the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025: Asst. Engineer (Civil), Jr. Planner, Asst. Engineer (Electrical), Asst. Sthapathi, Executive Officer (Grade I), Asst. Engineer (Agriculture Eng), Jr. Electrical Inspector, Agricultural Officer (Ext), Asst. Director (Industrial Safety & Health), Inspector of Fisheries, Environmental Scientist, Librarian (Grade II), Research Assistant, Statistical Inspector, Jr. Scientific Officer, Jr. Manager (Personal Admin), Jr. Manager (Finance & Accounts), Computer Programmer, Jr. Manager (Technical), Asst. Superintendent of Vigilance Institutions, Translator (Cell), Systems Manager, Asst. Section Officer (Translation), Sr. Accountant, Asst. Manager (Mines), Project Officer, Executive (P&A), Statistical Asst., Mining Engineer, Asst. Engineer (Mech), Jr. Chemist, Community Officer, Asst. Statistical Investigator, Librarian, Asst. Tourist Officer (Grade II) and Computer-cum-Vaccine Store Keeper.

Which TNPSC exam has only one stage of exam and no interview?

In the wide list of exams conducted by the TNPSC, the TNPSC Group 4 Exam and the TNPSC Combined Technical Services Non-Interview Exam are single stage exam. They have only one written exam and do not have any interview.

What is the minimum age to apply for the TNPSC Combined Technical Services Non-Interview Exam 2025?

Candidates should have completed 21 years of age for all posts mentioned in the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025, except for the post of Executive Officer (Grade I). For the post of Executive Officer (Grade I), candidates should have completed 30 years of age.

How many non-interview posts were announed as vacancies through TNPSC Combined Technical Services Non-Interview Exam 2025?

A total of 615 non-interview vacancies were announced through the TNPSC Combined Technical Services (Non-Interview) Exam 2025.
